OUR ONLINE DESIGN JOURNEY
AND WHAT TO EXPECT?

A simple three phase process that guides you closer to your spirit home

BRIEFING

The seeding phase

The planting of our working relationship. It is here, that we uncover your uniqueness through the personality questionnaire and free one to one phone or video call. Where you can share a mood board of your passions, ideas and core wishes.

CONCEPT

The flowering phase

The budding of creativity and artistic imagination, in this phase we will bring your ideas and dreams to life. After creating an array of thoughtfully designed suggestions, we will choose your favourite items and source at least three options for each. Which then flowers through to your Illustrated styling board and 3D visualisation renderings of each room.

FINAL DETAIL

The blossoming phase

The final touches in full bloom. Once we have designed a concept that emanates who you are, we will construct the technical drawings. These designs will be inclusive of bespoke furniture, joinery, interior architecture and a shopping list with links for you to execute purchasing.
